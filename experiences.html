My Experience

Hi! This is my experiences page, specifically listing experiences relevant to my career aspirations of becoming a Software Engineer.

At the start of my college career I knew I wanted to gain familiarity in working in the industry as a software engineer before actually graduating. This is why last summer I worked at my first internship at CarGurus as a Backend Software Engineer! Beyond all of the technical expertise I gained, I had an amazing time getting to know my team as well as all of the other interns who were apart of CarGurus' internship program that year.

This was my first internship experience, however, since then, I've continued to work in the industry as both a Web Developer at a Boston-based Insurance Firm, and as a iOS Swift Developer at Nasdaq! I've been working at both companies throughout the current and previous semester, working part-time at both, and I've been loving it!

I've never worked as a Web Developer or iOS developer before, so both roles were incredibly fascinating and new to me which has allowed me to learn so much information over the past few months.

And that's it! Before working at CarGurus, I did actually work as a Scooper at a local ice cream joint in Boston, called J.P. Licks. And, before that, I worked as a Crew Member at Chipotle!
